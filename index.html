Silk Nodes is a modern organization running quality nodes for chains in the blockchain ecosystem.

We aim to be a trusted provider to help projects maintain and secure their blockchains. Thats why we pride ourselves on being 100% bare metal validators, running our own infrastructure to validate the chains we are active on, we are also proud to say we run IBC relayer nodes to help support the networks.
